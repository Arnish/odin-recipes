Chai

Chai is an Indian tea beverage made by boiling black tea in milk and water with a mixture of aromatic herbs and spices. Originating in India the beverage has gained worldwide popularity, becoming a feature in many coffee and tea houses.

What you'll need:

  • Any black Tea (I use Assam and earl grey).
  • Whole Spices (Cardamom, cloves, peppercorn, cinnamon, and star anise).
  • Milk of preference, I use oat milk!
  • Sweetener

Steps:

  1. Crush the spices using a grinder.
  2. Add the spices and a cup of water to your pot. Bring to a simmer
  3. Add the tea and milk then bring it to a boil. Make sure to keep stirring!
  4. Lastly add your sweetener, strain and serve!
